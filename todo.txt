2D Zombie Game with Rust and SDL2
1. World Generation
  - Randomly generated cities/roads/houses/forests with loot
  - Special blocks for crafting, cooking, etc
2. GUI
  - HUD for health, hunger, thirst, strength
  - Debuffs and buffs
  - Minimap
  - Hotbar
  - Information on mouse hover
3. Player
  - Line of sight
  - Range and melee attacks
  - Inventory system
  - Movement - crouching vs walking vs running
  - Crafting/Building?
4. Enemies
  - Spawn in groups, generated with world
  - Attraction to noise
